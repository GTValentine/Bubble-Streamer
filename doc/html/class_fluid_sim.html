Detailed Description

Grid-based fluid solver.

Grid-based fluid solver based on Fluid3D library by Christopher Batty and a book "Fluid Simulation for Computer Graphics" by Robert Bridson. It solves a rectangular tank, full of fluid (no free surface) of varying density. It's assumed that left bottom near corner of the tank is located at (0, 0, 0)
